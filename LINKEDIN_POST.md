built a tiny AI company inside my company.

17 agents. 4 cron jobs. runs while i sleep.

content engine writes LinkedIn posts. competitor monitor tracks 12 rivals daily. SEO pipeline generates keyword briefs + schema. WhatsApp blast manages broadcast lists.

stack: hermes agent + omniroute (692 models local gateway) + fastapi + supabase. near-zero API cost.

it's not perfect. agents hallucinate. cron jobs die at 3am. prompts rot monthly. retry logic held together with hope.

but cheaper than a full-time hire and doesn't take sick days.

still building. still breaking things. documenting everything as i go.

if you're a solo founder trying to do more with less — this is the only way i've found that actually compounds.
